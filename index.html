How to Use the HyNa Kiosk

Step 1: Start the Session

Walk up to the HyNa kiosk and simply tap the print button. The machine will instantly get ready for your session and display a QR code.

Step 2: Scan the QR

Scan the QR code shown on the kiosk screen using your phone. It takes you directly to a secure upload page where you can submit your file.

Step 3: Upload & Print

Choose your file, pay securely online, and let the kiosk handle the rest. Your document will be printed immediately with no staff needed.
