Start Your Own Vending Business!

HyNa provides free high-tech printing vending machines to qualifying institutions such as colleges, universities, and public service venues across Kerala. As a franchise partner, you manage the business, earn 100% of the print revenue, and benefit from full backend support.

Why Become a HyNa Franchisee?

      Zero Machine Cost: We provide the vending machine at no upfront hardware cost.
        Earn Passive Income: You receive all earnings from print jobs directly to your bank account.
          Fully Supported: Our team handles setup, branding, digital payment onboarding, and training.
            Smart Software: Track prints, refill levels, and revenue in real-time from anywhere.
              Exclusive Ad Display Rights: We retain advertisement screen rights to support operations.

Who Can Apply?

We’re currently onboarding franchisees across educational and commercial locations in Kerala. Ideal candidates include:

    College campuses & administrators Small business owners or entrepreneurs Office parks and co-working spaces Hospitals, railway stations, and transport hubs

Selection Criteria

As we invest heavily in each machine, only select partners
are accepted based on the following:

High footfall and secure location,Internet and power availability Willingness to maintain the machine and refill consumables Agreement to allow company branding and advertisement display

How It Works

      Apply: Fill the franchise interest form. We'll assess your location and requirements.
        Approval: If approved, you’ll sign a simple MOU and confirm your interest.
          Install: We deliver the machine, set it up, and connect it to payment gateway.
            Operate: You’ll receive training, product refilling info, and start earning immediately.

Financial Breakdown

      Initial Setup: ₹0 machine cost (conditions apply)
        Product Refills: You’ll refill paper and cartridges (~₹800–₹1500/month depending on usage)
          Monthly Earnings: Depends on location, avg. ₹5k–₹20k/month for active machines
            Advertisement Revenue: Not available to franchisee; retained by company

Included Services

      Machine branding + UV vinyl design
        Installation support
          Razorpay/UPI payment integration
            Online dashboard access for earnings and print status
              Ongoing support via WhatsApp/email

Franchise Owner Responsibilities

      Provide reliable power and internet connection
        Maintain machine hygiene and security
          Refill paper and cartridges as needed
            Check dashboard for low supplies and errors
